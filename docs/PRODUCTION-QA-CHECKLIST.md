# Kastalabs Production QA Checklist

Use this before launch or deployment review.

## SEO

- Confirm one SEO plugin decision: Rank Math or Yoast.
- Verify meta title and meta description for Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Insights, and Contact.
- Validate JSON-LD with Rich Results Test:
  - Organization
  - WebSite
  - CreativeWork for Portfolio detail
  - Article for Insight detail
- Confirm sitemap is generated by the chosen SEO plugin.
- Confirm `/work/` legacy route strategy: keep temporarily, redirect, or remove after migration.

## Security

- Confirm contact form nonce validation works.
- Confirm contact form honeypot blocks bot-like submissions.
- Confirm rate limiting rejects repeated submissions after threshold.
- Verify security headers:
  - `X-Content-Type-Options`
  - `X-Frame-Options`
  - `Referrer-Policy`
  - `Permissions-Policy`
- Confirm `wp-config.php`, uploads, build output, and local artifacts are ignored.

## Performance

- Run Lighthouse on desktop and mobile for:
  - `/`
  - `/services/`
  - `/portfolio/`
  - `/contact/`
- Target 90+ where realistic.
- Confirm CSS/JS bundles are loaded from the Vite manifest.
- Confirm above-the-fold CSS is not blocked by missing build assets.
- Confirm images use proper dimensions and compressed formats.

## Accessibility

- Check keyboard navigation for header, CTA buttons, forms, filters, and pagination.
- Confirm focus states are visible.
- Confirm form labels are programmatic labels.
- Confirm text contrast on navy CTA and soft blue surfaces.
- Confirm heading hierarchy is logical per page.
